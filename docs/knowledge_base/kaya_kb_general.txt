FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================

Q: How many Kaya clinics are there in India?
A: Kaya has 80+ clinics across 27+ cities in India, plus 23 clinics in the Middle East.

Q: Is there a consultation fee?
A: A basic consultation is completely free. If a detailed medical evaluation is required, the fee is Rs. 750. The dermatologist will confirm before any charges apply.

Q: Is Kaya suitable for all skin types?
A: Yes. Kaya's treatments and products are designed for all skin types — oily, dry, combination, sensitive, and normal — with formulations developed specifically for Indian skin.

Q: How many sessions does Laser Hair Reduction require?
A: Multiple sessions are typically needed for long-lasting results. The exact number depends on the treatment area, hair type, and skin type. The dermatologist will advise during consultation.

Q: Are Kaya treatments safe?
A: Yes. All treatments use US FDA-approved technology and are administered by qualified dermatologists and trained practitioners.

Q: Does Kaya sell products online?
A: Yes. The full range is available at www.kaya.in/all-products.

Q: What is the difference between PRP and QR678 for hair loss?
A: PRP uses the patient's own blood plasma to stimulate hair follicles. QR678 uses bioengineered growth factors to repair damaged follicles. Both are available at Kaya — the dermatologist recommends the best option based on the individual's hair loss pattern.

Q: What is HIFU?
A: HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a non-surgical skin tightening and lifting treatment. It stimulates deep collagen production with no surgery or downtime, treating sagging skin, wrinkles, and fine lines.

Q: Does Kaya have clinics outside India?
A: Yes, Kaya has 23 clinics in the Middle East.

Q: What is the Kaya Nutra+ range?
A: Kaya Nutra+ is Kaya's nutraceutical range — ingestible supplements for skin and hair health, including Collagen Powder and supplements under the Beautywise brand.

Q: Can men visit Kaya?
A: Absolutely. Kaya provides treatments and products for both men and women, including specialised hair loss treatments for men.

Q: How do I find the nearest clinic?
A: Visit kaya.in/dermatologist-near-me or call 1800-209-5292.

Q: What is CoolSculpting?
A: CoolSculpting is a non-invasive body contouring treatment that uses controlled cooling to freeze and eliminate stubborn fat cells, with no surgery or downtime.
