SKIN SERVICES
================================================================

Kaya offers advanced skin treatments delivered by qualified dermatologists, customised to individual skin types and concerns.

LASER HAIR REDUCTION
Description: Permanent solution for unwanted hair using FDA-approved laser technology. Targets hair follicles to prevent regrowth without harming the skin.
Areas treated: Face, underarms, legs, back, arms, and more.
Benefits: Safe for all skin types, permanent reduction over multiple sessions, minimal discomfort, no downtime.

CHEMICAL PEEL TREATMENT
Description: Exfoliates dead skin cells, reduces blemishes, and treats pigmentation. Promotes regeneration of new cells and improves skin texture.
Treats: Acne scars, fine lines, dullness, pigmentation, uneven skin tone.
Benefits: Customised peel intensity, refreshed complexion, minimal downtime.

SKIN BRIGHTENING & LIGHTENING
Description: Targets dullness, pigmentation, and uneven skin tone using advanced lasers and brightening facials.
Benefits: Eliminates dark spots, promotes even complexion, non-invasive, suitable for all skin types.

Q-SWITCH LASER TREATMENT
Description: Targets pigmentation, dark spots, and uneven skin tone using laser energy to break down excess melanin.
Treats: Freckles, age spots, sun damage, melasma.
Benefits: Safe, non-invasive, visible brightening after a few sessions.

FACIAL TREATMENTS
Types: Anti-acne facials, hydrating facials, anti-ageing facials.
Benefits: Deep pore cleansing, removal of impurities, revitalised glowing skin.

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound)
Description: Non-surgical facelift. Stimulates collagen production to firm and rejuvenate skin.
Treats: Sagging skin, jowls, fine lines, neck laxity.
Benefits: No surgery, no downtime, long-lasting results, improves skin elasticity.

ULTRACEL Q+ HIFU
Description: Latest generation HIFU for skin lifting and tightening. Targets multiple skin layers to stimulate collagen and elastin.
Treats: Sagging skin, double chin, wrinkles.
Benefits: No downtime, superior lifting, painless and non-invasive.

THERMAGE
Description: Radiofrequency technology to smooth, tighten, and contour skin. Stimulates collagen growth.
Treats: Fine lines, wrinkles, sagging skin on face, neck, and body.
Benefits: Non-surgical, minimal downtime, natural long-lasting results.

DERMAL FILLERS
Description: Instant volume and smoothness using safe medical-grade fillers. Non-surgical anti-ageing solution.
Areas: Cheeks, lips, under-eye, nasolabial folds.
Benefits: Immediate visible results, natural-looking, lasts up to 12 months, minimal discomfort.

ACNE SCARS TREATMENT
Description: Targeted treatments to reduce and eliminate acne scars and blemishes.

MICRONEEDLING
Description: Stimulates natural collagen production through controlled micro-injuries. Improves skin texture and reduces scars.

ANTI-WRINKLE TREATMENT
Description: Advanced solutions to reduce and prevent fine lines and wrinkles.

UNDER-EYE / EYE CIRCLE TREATMENT
Description: Specialised treatments for dark circles, puffiness, and under-eye concerns.

HAIR SERVICES
================================================================

Kaya offers comprehensive hair loss and scalp treatments delivered by expert dermatologists.

HAIR LOSS TREATMENT
Description: Personalised solutions for hair thinning and hair fall. Experts diagnose the root cause and recommend treatments from topical applications to advanced therapies.
For: Men and women experiencing hair loss or thinning.

PRP TREATMENT (Platelet-Rich Plasma)
Description: Stimulates hair growth using the patient's own natural growth factors. Minimally invasive procedure that revitalises dormant hair follicles.
Benefits: Boosts hair density and thickness, improves scalp circulation, safe (uses body's own plasma).

QR678 THERAPY (Biocell Therapy)
Description: Uses bioengineered growth factors to repair and rejuvenate hair follicles. Revitalises scalp health and strengthens hair roots.
Benefits: Promotes natural hair growth, reduces hair fall, improves hair texture, long-term results.

HAIR THREAD TREATMENT (PDO Threading)
Description: Bio-absorbable threads inserted into the scalp to stimulate collagen and improve blood flow to follicles.
Benefits: Non-surgical, thicker healthier hair, improves hair density.

HAIR LOSS TREATMENT FOR MEN
Description: Specialised treatments for male pattern baldness and hair thinning.

HAIR LOSS TREATMENT FOR WOMEN
Description: Tailored solutions for female hair thinning, hair fall, and female pattern hair loss.

SCALP CARE SOLUTIONS
Description: Treats dandruff, dryness, and itchiness. Advanced scalp therapies cleanse and nourish the scalp.
Benefits: Creates ideal environment for hair growth, revitalises hair follicles.
