SECTION 11: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)
================================================================

Q: How many Kaya clinics are there in India?
A: Kaya has 80+ clinics across 27+ cities in India. There are also 23 clinics in the Middle East.

Q: How do I book an appointment at Kaya?
A: You can book an appointment by calling the toll-free number 1800-209-5292, by visiting www.kaya.in/kaya-book-an-appointment, or by walking into your nearest Kaya clinic.

Q: Is Kaya suitable for all skin types?
A: Yes. Kaya's treatments and products are designed for all skin types including oily, dry, combination, sensitive, and normal. The formulations are specially developed for Indian skin.

Q: How many sessions does Laser Hair Reduction require?
A: Laser Hair Reduction at Kaya typically requires multiple sessions for long-lasting results. The exact number depends on the treatment area, hair type, and skin type. A dermatologist will advise during consultation.

Q: Are Kaya treatments safe?
A: Yes. All Kaya treatments use US FDA-approved technology and conform to the highest international quality and safety standards. Treatments are administered by qualified dermatologists and trained skin practitioners.

Q: Does Kaya sell skincare products online?
A: Yes. Kaya's full range of skincare, haircare, and bath & body products can be purchased at www.kaya.in/all-products.

Q: What is the difference between PRP and QR678 Therapy for hair loss?
A: PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) uses the patient's own blood plasma to stimulate hair follicles. QR678 Therapy uses bioengineered growth factors to repair damaged follicles and promote hair growth. Both are available at Kaya and a dermatologist will recommend the best option based on your hair loss pattern.

Q: What is HIFU treatment?
A: HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a non-surgical skin tightening and lifting treatment. It uses ultrasound energy to stimulate deep collagen production without any surgery or downtime. It effectively treats sagging skin, wrinkles, and fine lines.

Q: Is there a consultation fee at Kaya?
A: For accurate pricing and consultation fee information, it is best to contact Kaya directly at 1800-209-5292 or visit www.kaya.in.

Q: Does Kaya have clinics outside India?
A: Yes. Kaya has 23 clinics in the Middle East in addition to its 80+ clinics across India.

Q: What is the Kaya Nutra+ range?
A: Kaya Nutra+ is Kaya's nutraceutical range — ingestible supplements designed to support skin and hair health from within. It includes products like Collagen Powder and supplements under the Beautywise brand.

Q: Can men visit Kaya for treatments?
A: Absolutely. Kaya provides treatments and products for both men and women, including specialised hair loss treatments for men and skincare ranges for all genders.

Q: How do I find the nearest Kaya clinic?
A: Visit kaya.in/dermatologist-near-me and select your city, or call 1800-209-5292 and the team will help you locate the nearest clinic.

Q: What is CoolSculpting?
